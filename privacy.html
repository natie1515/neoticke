User Privacy Rights

🚫 What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not collect passwords.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not access private Discord messages unless explicitly provided through a ticket.
🏢

Data Controller

The NeoTicket team is responsible for processing information necessary for operating the bot and providing support services.

🆔

Discord Data

User IDs, server IDs, role IDs, channel IDs, ticket IDs and configuration settings may be stored.

🎫

Ticket Data

Ticket status, creation date, closure date, assigned staff, categories and support information.

💬

Messages & Content

Messages, attachments, images and links may be included in ticket transcripts when transcript features are enabled.

⚙️

Server Configuration

Support roles, categories, panels, welcome messages, logging channels and automation settings.

🛡️

Anti Abuse

Information may be processed to prevent spam, ticket flooding, abuse and malicious activity.

📁

Transcripts

Ticket transcripts may be generated and stored according to server configuration choices.

🌍

Third Party Services

Discord, hosting providers, databases and cloud services may process information necessary for operation.

Data Retention

⚙️ Configuration Data

Stored while the bot remains active in the server.

🎫 Active Tickets

Stored until tickets are closed or removed.

📁 Ticket Transcripts

Retention depends on administrator settings.

🛡️ Security Logs

May be retained for abuse prevention and auditing.

⚖️ Your Rights

  • Request access to stored information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion where applicable.
  • Request additional information regarding data processing.
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns.
